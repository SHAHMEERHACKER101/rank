Privacy Policy

Last updated: August 26, 2025

Introduction

NexusRank Pro ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our AI-powered SEO toolkit service.

Information We Collect

Information You Provide

  • Text Content: Content you submit to our AI tools for processing
  • Account Information: When you upgrade to Pro (username, password)
  • Contact Information: Email address when you contact support

Automatically Collected Information

  • Usage Data: Tool usage counts stored locally in your browser
  • Technical Data: IP address, browser type, device information
  • Performance Data: Service response times and error logs

How We Use Your Information

  • Service Provision: Processing your text through AI models
  • Usage Tracking: Managing free usage limits and Pro access
  • Service Improvement: Analyzing performance and fixing issues
  • Communication: Responding to support requests
  • Security: Preventing abuse and maintaining service integrity

Data Retention and Storage

Privacy-First Approach

We do NOT store your content. All text you submit for AI processing is immediately discarded after generating results. Your content never touches our databases.

What We Store

  • Usage Counts: Stored locally in your browser only
  • Pro Status: Stored locally in your browser only
  • Technical Logs: Anonymized error logs for 30 days maximum
  • Support Communications: Contact form submissions for 1 year

Third-Party Services

Google Gemini AI

We use Google's Gemini AI to process your content. Your text is sent to Google's servers for processing and immediately discarded. Google's privacy policy applies to this processing.

Cloudflare

Our service is hosted on Cloudflare, which may process technical data for security and performance purposes. Cloudflare's privacy policy applies to this processing.

CDN Services

We use content delivery networks (Google Fonts, Font Awesome) for faster loading. These services may collect basic technical information.

Data Security

  • Encryption: All data transmission uses HTTPS encryption
  • Access Control: Strict access controls on our infrastructure
  • Regular Audits: Security reviews and vulnerability assessments
  • Incident Response: Procedures for handling security incidents

Your Rights

Data Control

  • Access: Request information about data we may have
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account data
  • Correction: Update incorrect information
  • Portability: Export your data in machine-readable format

Browser Controls

  • Clear Local Data: Clear browser storage to reset usage counts
  • Disable Cookies: Control cookie preferences in your browser
  • Private Browsing: Use incognito mode for enhanced privacy

International Data Transfers

Our services operate globally through Cloudflare's network. Your data may be processed in countries outside your residence. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place for international transfers, including adherence to applicable data protection laws.

Children's Privacy

Our service is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date. Your continued use of the service after changes constitutes acceptance of the new policy.
