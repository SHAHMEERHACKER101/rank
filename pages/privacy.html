Privacy Policy

How we collect, use, and protect your information

Last updated: August 25, 2025

1. Information We Collect

Information You Provide

  • Text Content: Text you submit to our AI tools for processing
  • Account Information: Username and password for Pro accounts
  • Usage Data: Information about how you use our tools and services
  • Contact Information: Email address and other details when you contact us

Automatically Collected Information

  • Device Information: Browser type, operating system, device identifiers
  • Usage Analytics: Pages visited, features used, time spent on site
  • Local Storage: Usage counters and preferences stored in your browser
  • Cookies: Essential cookies for site functionality

2. How We Use Your Information

Service Provision

Process your text through our AI tools and deliver results

Service Improvement

Analyze usage patterns to enhance our tools and features

Account Management

Manage your Pro account and track usage limits

Communication

Respond to your inquiries and provide support

3. Data Storage and Security

Encryption

All data transmission is encrypted using industry-standard SSL/TLS protocols.

Secure Infrastructure

Our services run on Cloudflare's secure infrastructure with global edge locations.

Data Retention

Processed text is not permanently stored and is deleted after processing completion.

Privacy by Design

We minimize data collection and process only what's necessary for our services.

4. Third-Party Services

DeepSeek AI

We use DeepSeek's AI API to process your text. Your content is sent securely to DeepSeek for processing and is not stored by them beyond the processing session.

Cloudflare

Our website and services are hosted on Cloudflare's platform, which provides security, performance, and analytics services.

Patreon

Pro subscriptions are managed through Patreon. Their privacy policy governs payment and subscription data.

5. Your Rights and Choices

Access

Request information about the data we have collected about you

Correction

Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete information

Deletion

Request deletion of your personal information (subject to legal requirements)

Opt-out

Opt out of certain data processing activities

6. Cookies and Local Storage

Essential Cookies

We use essential cookies and local storage to:

  • Remember your usage limits for free tools
  • Maintain your Pro account session
  • Store your preferences and settings
  • Ensure proper website functionality

Managing Cookies

You can control cookies through your browser settings, but disabling them may affect site functionality. Local storage data remains on your device and can be cleared through your browser.

7. Children's Privacy

Our services are not designed for or directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we learn that we have collected such information, we will delete it promptly.

8. International Data Transfers

Your information may be processed and stored on servers located outside your country of residence. We ensure that such transfers comply with applicable data protection laws and implement appropriate safeguards.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by posting the updated policy on our website and updating the "Last updated" date. Your continued use of our services constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
