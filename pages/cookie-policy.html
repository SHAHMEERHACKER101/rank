Cookie Policy

How we use cookies and similar technologies

Last updated: August 25, 2025

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device (computer, tablet, or mobile) when you visit our website. They help us provide you with a better experience by remembering your preferences and understanding how you use our service.

Important: We only use essential cookies and local storage that are necessary for our service to function properly. We do not use tracking cookies for advertising purposes.

Types of Cookies We Use

Essential Cookies

Always Active

These cookies are necessary for the website to function and cannot be switched off in our systems. They are usually only set in response to actions made by you.

What we use essential cookies for:

  • Remembering your usage limits for free tools
  • Maintaining your Pro account session
  • Storing your preferences and settings
  • Ensuring security and preventing fraud
  • Making our website work properly

Analytics Cookies

Optional

These cookies help us understand how visitors interact with our website by collecting and reporting information anonymously.

What we use analytics cookies for:

  • Understanding which tools are most popular
  • Identifying technical issues and bugs
  • Improving our website performance
  • Measuring the effectiveness of our content

Note: We currently do not use analytics cookies, but may implement them in the future with your consent.

Local Storage

In addition to cookies, we use browser local storage to store information that enhances your experience with our tools.

What We Store Locally:

Usage Counters

Track how many times you've used each free tool (2 uses per tool limit)

Pro Status

Remember if you're logged in as a Pro user for unlimited access

Preferences

Store your interface preferences and settings

View Cloudflare Cookie Policy

View Patreon Cookie Policy

Managing Your Cookie Preferences

Browser Settings

You can control and delete cookies through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to:

  • View what cookies are stored on your device
  • Delete existing cookies
  • Block cookies from specific websites
  • Block all cookies (may affect website functionality)

Mobile Devices

On mobile devices, you can manage cookies through your browser app settings:

  • iOS Safari: Settings > Safari > Block All Cookies
  • Android Chrome: Chrome menu > Settings > Privacy > Cookies
  • Android Firefox: Firefox menu > Settings > Privacy > Cookies

Local Storage

To clear local storage data for our website:

  • Use your browser's developer tools (F12) and navigate to Application/Storage
  • Use browser settings to clear website data
  • Clear all browsing data (this affects all websites)

Important Note

Disabling or clearing cookies and local storage may affect the functionality of our website. You may lose your usage tracking and Pro login status, requiring you to log in again.

Cookie Consent

By continuing to use our website, you consent to our use of essential cookies and local storage as described in this policy. We will ask for your explicit consent before using any non-essential cookies in the future.

Your Rights:

  • You can withdraw your consent at any time by clearing cookies
  • You can request information about what data we store
  • You can ask us to delete your stored data
  • You can opt out of any future non-essential cookies

Data Retention

Essential Cookies

Stored until you clear them or they expire (typically when you close your browser)

Local Storage

Stored indefinitely until you clear them or we update our storage format

Pro Login

Pro status is stored locally until you log out or clear browser data
