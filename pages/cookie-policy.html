What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your device when you visit our website. They help us provide you with a better experience by remembering your preferences and analyzing how you use our service.

How We Use Cookies

Minimal Cookie Usage

NexusRank Pro uses minimal cookies and relies primarily on local browser storage for functionality. We prioritize your privacy and avoid unnecessary tracking.

Cookie Categories

Essential Cookies

Required for the website to function properly. These cannot be disabled as they are necessary for core functionality like usage tracking and Pro access.

Functional Cookies

Enhance your experience by remembering preferences and improving performance. These can be disabled in your browser settings.

Analytics Cookies

Currently not used. If implemented in the future, these would help us understand how users interact with our service to improve functionality.

Local Storage vs Cookies

What We Store Locally

  • nexusrank_usage: Tracks how many times you've used each tool
  • nexusrank_pro: Stores your Pro user status

Benefits of Local Storage

  • Data stays on your device and is not sent to servers
  • Faster access and better performance
  • More storage capacity than traditional cookies
  • You have full control over this data

Third-Party Services

We use Google Fonts to provide better typography. Google may collect technical information when fonts are loaded. You can block this by disabling web fonts in your browser or using a content blocker.

Our service is hosted on Cloudflare, which may use cookies for security, performance optimization, and DDoS protection. These cookies are essential for service delivery.

Cookie Consent

By using our website, you consent to our use of essential cookies and local storage as described in this policy. For non-essential cookies from third-party services, you can manage these through your browser settings.

Withdrawal of Consent

  • Clear cookies and local storage in your browser
  • Use private/incognito browsing mode
  • Configure browser to block cookies
  • Use content blockers or ad blockers
