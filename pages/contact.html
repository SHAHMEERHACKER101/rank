Frequently Asked Questions

How do I upgrade to Pro access?

You can upgrade to Pro access by visiting our Patreon page and subscribing to our Pro tier. Once subscribed, you'll receive login credentials for unlimited access to all tools.

What's included in the free tier?

The free tier includes 2 uses per tool without registration. This allows you to test all our AI-powered tools including the SEO Writer, Humanizer, AI Detector, Paraphrasing Tool, Grammar Checker, and Text Improver.

How accurate is the AI Detector?

Our AI Detector achieves high accuracy rates by using advanced machine learning models. However, AI detection is an evolving field, and no detector is 100% accurate. We recommend using the results as guidance rather than definitive proof.

Is my content stored on your servers?

No, we don't permanently store your content. Text is processed through our AI models and then discarded. We prioritize your privacy and data security. For more details, please review our Privacy Policy.

Can I use the generated content commercially?

Yes, you own the rights to content generated using our tools. However, we recommend reviewing and editing AI-generated content to ensure it meets your quality standards and doesn't infringe on any third-party rights.

What languages do you support?

Currently, our tools work best with English content. We're working on expanding language support and plan to add multiple languages in future updates. Stay tuned for announcements!
